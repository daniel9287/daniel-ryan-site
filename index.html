About Me

I am a molecular microbiologist specializing in RNA-based regulatory mechanisms in gut microbial ecosystems. My research integrates microbiology, biochemistry, and computational methodologies to investigate host–microbe interactions at the molecular level. As of April 1st, 2025, I have been appointed as a Group Leader at the Institute of Biochemistry and Biophysics, Polish Academy of Sciences. In parallel with my academic pursuits, I provide professional consulting services in the following areas: (a) strategic planning and development of competitive research grants, (b) laboratory setup and operational management, (c) RNA sequencing and bioinformatic analysis, and (d) transcriptome annotation and interpretation.
